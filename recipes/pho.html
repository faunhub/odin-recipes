How to make Pho

Description

This is a step-by-step recipe on how to make Pho by yourself.

Pho comes from Northen Vietnam. In this tutorial on how to make pho from scratch, you can explore the tedious process behind the work it takes to make this distinctive broth that is both so light and full of flavor at the same time.

Ingredients

  • Store bought Pho noodles
  • Beef:
    • 1.5kg brisket
    • 1kg beef bones
  • Spices:
    • star anise
    • innamon quills
    • cardamon pods
  • Onions
  • Beansprouts

Steps

  1. Rinse bone and boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Bring 3.5 liters to boil and add everything.
  3. Season both.
  4. Boil noodles right before serving.
  5. Put Beansprouts on top for garnish.
