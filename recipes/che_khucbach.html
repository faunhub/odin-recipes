How to make Che Khuc Bach

Description

A light and refreshing Vietnamese dessert. Che (there are a variety of Ches) khuc bach is easy to make at home to enjoy with your family.

It is a great summer dish, especially to cool off if you live in a tropical climate like Vietnam. It is also a very popular street snack amongst middle and high school students.

Ingredients

  • Cow milk
  • Gelatin
  • Heavy cream
  • Fruits (your choice of fruits)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Almond slices for garnish

Steps

  1. Pour 300ml cow milk with 21g gelatin powder and let it sit for 30 minutes, then stir.
  2. Add 60g sugar, 300ml heavy cream in the mixture, continue to stir.
  3. Let it sit in the fridge for about 2 hours.
  4. Dice fruits if needed.
  5. Bring to boil 800ml of water, then put in 50g of sugar. Then add juice from canned fruits.
  6. Take Khuc bach mixture from the fridge out, cut into small square pieces.
  7. Use together with diced fruits, sugar water.
  8. Add almond slices for texture and garnish.
